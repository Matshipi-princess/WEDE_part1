Become a Vendor at Munch & Makers!

Join our community of passionate local growers, makers, and groups.

Our Vision

We're looking for vendors who share our passion for high-quality, local goods. By joining our market, you become part of a movement that supports small businesses and connects with the community.

Our Stalls

Whether you sell delicious baked goods, fresh produce, or unique artisan crafts, our platform is designed to help your business thrive. We provide the tools to reach a wider audience and grow your brand.

How It Works

We make it easy to sell online. Simply apply, set up your stall, and we'll handle the logistics, from marketing your products to arranging convenient delivery options for your customers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What kind of products can I sell?

A: We welcome a wide range of local products, including fresh produce, baked goods, prepared foods, handmade crafts, and more. All products should be locally sourced or made.

Q: How often do I need to attend the market?

A: Our market offers flexible attendance options. You can choose to participate weekly, bi-weekly, or on a seasonal basis. Specific arrangements can be discussed during the application process.

Q: Are there any fees to become a vendor?

A: Yes, there is a small administrative fee to cover the costs of listing and managing your stall on our platform. Delivery fees also apply for doorstep delivery. Details will be provided upon approval of your application.

Q: How does the delivery system work?

A: We operate a network of local delivery partners. Once an order is placed through our online market, our team handles the collection from your stall and delivers directly to the customer's doorstep, ensuring freshness and efficiency.

Q: Can community groups participate?

A: Absolutely! We encourage local community groups to join us to raise awareness, share information, or fundraise. Please select "Community Group" in the stall category dropdown.
