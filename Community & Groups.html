Build Your Community Hub

At Munch & Makers Farmers Market, we believe the heart of a vibrant community beats strongest when people connect, share, and grow together. This is your space to forge new bonds, find like-minded individuals and create a thriving community right here in Johannesburg!

Create Your Own Group

Do you have a passion, a hobby, or a local initiative you want to champion? Why not start your own community group? Whether it's a gardening club, a book circle, a local history appreciation society or a support network for new parents we provide the platform to help you find your founding members and get your group off the ground.

It's an amazing way to gather people around a shared interest, organize local meetups, and truly enrich the lives of those around you.

To enquire:

Email: info@munchandmakers.com


Why should you create a Group?

  • Connect : Meet people who share your passions and values.
  • Grow : Learn new skills, share knowledge, and collaborate on projects.
  • Impact : Contribute to local initiatives and make a difference in Johannesburg.
  • Belong : Find a sense of camaraderie and support within your local network.
